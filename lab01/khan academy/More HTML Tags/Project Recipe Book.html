Recipe #1: Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: ~20 minutes
  • Serves: 2–3
  • Good for: breakfast
Ingredients Quantity
Flour1 cup
Milk1 cup
Egg1
Sugar1 tbsp
Baking powder2 tsp
Saltpinch
Butter or oilfor frying

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, salt.
  2. Add milk and egg, whisk until smooth (don’t overmix).
  3. Heat a pan, lightly oil it.
  4. Pour batter, cook 1–2 minutes, flip, cook another 1 minute.
  5. Serve with honey, berries, or jam.

Source: Wikipedia (Pancake)

Recipe #2: Simple Chicken Salad

  • Time: ~15 minutes (if chicken is cooked)
  • Serves: 2
  • Good for: lunch
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked chicken200 g
Cucumber1
Tomatoes2
Lettucea handful
Olive oil1–2 tbsp
Lemon juice1 tbsp
Salt & pepperto taste

Steps

  1. Slice cucumber and tomatoes, tear lettuce.
  2. Cut chicken into pieces.
  3. Mix everything in a bowl.
  4. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately.

Recipe #3: Hot Chocolate

  • Time: ~10 minutes
  • Serves: 1–2
  • Good for: cozy evenings
Ingredients Quantity
Milk2 cups
Cocoa powder2 tbsp
Sugar1–2 tbsp
Chocolate (optional)30–50 g
Vanilla (optional)pinch

Steps

  1. Warm milk in a small pot (don’t boil).
  2. Whisk in cocoa and sugar until smooth.
  3. If using chocolate, add it and stir until melted.
  4. Pour into cups. Optional: marshmallows on top.

Source: Wikipedia (Hot chocolate)
